    				<p>This set of Food Engineering Multiple Choice Questions &#038; Answers (MCQs) focuses on &#8220;Characteristics and Composition of Food&#8221;.</p>
<p>1. Which of the following deals with how food is adjudged by the consumer?<br />
a) Food microbiology<br />
b) Product Development<br />
c) Sensory Analysis<br />
d) Food physics<br />
<span class="collapseomatic " id="id9655"  tabindex="" title="View Answer">View Answer</span><div id="target-id9655" class="collapseomatic_content ">Answer: c<br />
Explanation: Sensory Analysis deals with how the food is adjudged by a consumer.</div>

	<p>2. Industry lobbying, local to international rules, Quality and food safety etc includes which component of the food industry?<br />
a) R&#038;D<br />
b) Financial Services<br />
c) Regulation<br />
d) Wholesale and distribution<br />
<span class="collapseomatic " id="id4696"  tabindex="" title="View Answer">View Answer</span><div id="target-id4696" class="collapseomatic_content ">Answer: c<br />
Explanation: Food regulation ensures Industry lobbying, local to international rules, Quality and food safety. </div>
<p>3. Which of the following does NOT constitute 90% of dry weight of any food?<br />
a) Carbohydrates<br />
b) Fibers<br />
c) Proteins<br />
d) Fats<br />
<span class="collapseomatic " id="id9708"  tabindex="" title="View Answer">View Answer</span><div id="target-id9708" class="collapseomatic_content ">Answer: b<br />
Explanation: Carbohydrates, fats and protein constitute 90% of dry weight of any food. </div>
<p>4. Which sentence is untrue with respect to the human body?<br />
a) Unconsumed water broken → fats<br />
b) Carbohydrates broken → Sugars<br />
c) Proteins broken down → Amino acids<br />
d) Fats broken down → Fatty acids and glycerol<br />
<span class="collapseomatic " id="id9780"  tabindex="" title="View Answer">View Answer</span><div id="target-id9780" class="collapseomatic_content ">Answer: a<br />
Explanation: Water isn&#8217;t converted into anything inside the body. It is just a carrier and removes toxins from the body via sweat and urine. </div>
<p>5. Which provides energy very slowly?<br />
a) Carbohydrates<br />
b) Fats<br />
c) Proteins<br />
d) Fibers<br />
<span class="collapseomatic " id="id5801"  tabindex="" title="View Answer">View Answer</span><div id="target-id5801" class="collapseomatic_content ">Answer: b<br />
Explanation: Carbohydrates provide quick energy. Proteins are for body growth. Fibers do not provide energy as they don&#8217;t have any calorific value. Hence, fats slowly provide energy. </div>

	<p>6. Which of the following is untrue?<br />
a) A gram of carbohydrate or protein contains 4 calories<br />
b) A gram of fat contains 9 calories<br />
c) A gram of fat contains 5 calories<br />
d) None of the mentioned<br />
<span class="collapseomatic " id="id3337"  tabindex="" title="View Answer">View Answer</span><div id="target-id3337" class="collapseomatic_content ">Answer: c<br />
Explanation: 4 calories make a gram of carbohydrate or protein and 9 calories make a gram of fat. </div>
<p>7. Storage requirements and stability, product attributes conductive to product sale etc. The above activities refer to which step of the food industry?<br />
a) Production<br />
b) Manufacture<br />
c) Distribution<br />
d) Marketing<br />
<span class="collapseomatic " id="id800"  tabindex="" title="View Answer">View Answer</span><div id="target-id800" class="collapseomatic_content ">Answer: c<br />
Explanation: Storage requirements and storage stability, product attributes conductive to product sale etc refer to Distribution of food products. </div>
<p>8. With respect to the definition of an allied industry, which is an allied industry with the food industry?<br />
a) Explosives Industry<br />
b) Packaging Industry<br />
c) Jute Industry<br />
d) Leather Industry<br />
<span class="collapseomatic " id="id398"  tabindex="" title="View Answer">View Answer</span><div id="target-id398" class="collapseomatic_content ">Answer: b<br />
Explanation: Non-food components and for marketing manufacturing companies are called allied industries. Packaging industry needed for food packaging is one of them. </div>
<p>9. Food industry is a high volume industry. Hence, any losses may be a major loss to the producer.<br />
a) True<br />
b) False<br />
<span class="collapseomatic " id="id7734"  tabindex="" title="View Answer">View Answer</span><div id="target-id7734" class="collapseomatic_content ">Answer: a<br />
Explanation: It is true that the food industry is a high volume industry. Hence, any loss is a major loss to the producer. </div>
<p>10. Increasing agricultural efficiency is the most important way to make sure food production meets the necessity.<br />
a) True<br />
b) False<br />
<span class="collapseomatic " id="id7112"  tabindex="" title="View Answer">View Answer</span><div id="target-id7112" class="collapseomatic_content ">Answer: a<br />
Explanation: Increasing the efficiency of agriculture is the most important way to make sure food production meets the demand. </div>
